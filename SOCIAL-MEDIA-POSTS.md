# Social Media Posts for Blog Articles

## üì± Platform-Specific Best Practices

### Facebook Posts
- **Optimal Length**: 40-80 characters for highest engagement
- **Max Length**: 250 characters (mobile cut-off)
- **Best Time to Post**: Tuesday-Thursday, 1-3 PM EST
- **Include**: Question or hook, benefit, link, call-to-action
- **Hashtags**: 1-3 relevant hashtags max (not over-used on Facebook)

### Google Business Posts
- **Optimal Length**: 100-300 characters
- **Max Length**: 1,500 characters (but most users won't read past 100)
- **Best Time to Post**: Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 5 PM local time
- **Include**: Local keywords, specific service, call-to-action
- **Update Frequency**: 2-3 posts per week minimum

### SEO Keywords to Include
- Primary: "contractor insurance", "workers compensation", "[state] contractor"
- Local: City/state names, "[trade] contractor [location]"
- Long-tail: "how much does [insurance type] cost", "[state] requirements"
- Industry: Specific trade names (HVAC, roofing, plumbing, electrical)

## üìä Performance Tracking

### Metrics to Monitor
- **Click-Through Rate (CTR)**: Target 2-5% on Facebook, 3-7% on Google Business
- **Engagement Rate**: Target 1-3% (likes, comments, shares combined)
- **Conversion Rate**: Target 5-10% (quote requests from clicks)

### A/B Testing Recommendations
Test these variations:
1. **Hook style**: Question vs. Statistic vs. Warning
2. **CTA placement**: Beginning vs. End
3. **Link format**: Full URL vs. Shortened vs. "Learn More"
4. **Hashtag count**: 1 vs. 2 vs. 3 hashtags
5. **Post length**: Short (40-80 chars) vs. Medium (100-150) vs. Long (200-250)

### Best Performing Post Formulas
1. **Stat + Fear + Solution**: "X% of contractors lose $Y | Avoid this mistake | Read guide"
2. **Question + Benefit**: "Are you compliant? | Learn requirements + save money"
3. **Warning + Urgency**: "New requirement affects [audience] | Deadline [date] | Full guide"
